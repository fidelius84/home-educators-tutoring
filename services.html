Key Stages

Tutoring at Every Stage

We support home-educated learners across all key stages. Each programme is built around the individual child, not a one-size-fits-all syllabus.

Early Years & Primary

AGES 5–11 · KEY STAGES 1 & 2
  • Phonics and early reading
  • Number bonds, times tables, mental maths
  • Sentence construction and creative writing
  • Introduction to science concepts
  • Confidence and curiosity building
  • SATs preparation (if applicable)

Secondary

AGES 11–16 · KEY STAGES 3 & 4
  • Full GCSE preparation (AQA, Edexcel, OCR)
  • Core and optional subject support
  • Essay writing and analytical skills
  • Algebra, geometry, statistics
  • Triple and Combined Science
  • Study skills and exam technique

Sixth Form & A-Level

AGES 16–18 · KEY STAGE 5
  • A-Level subject mastery
  • Extended essay and coursework support
  • Past paper and mark scheme practice
  • Exam timing and strategy
  • University application preparation
  • Subject-specific stretch and challenge
How We Work

Flexible Sessions, Consistent Progress

Every session is one-to-one. Your child receives their tutor's full attention, with a programme that adapts as they grow and develop.

Sessions are typically 60 minutes, though 45- and 90-minute formats are available for younger children or intensive revision periods.

After each lesson, parents receive a brief progress note covering what was covered, any areas for development, and suggested follow-up activities.

💻

Online Sessions

Delivered via video call using shared screen, interactive whiteboards, and digital resources. All you need is a stable internet connection.

🏠

In-Person Sessions

Available within our local area. Sessions take place at your home or another agreed safe location. Please contact us to confirm availability.

📋

Assessment & Planning

We begin with a short assessment to understand your child's current level, then build a structured plan with clear milestones.

📬

Parent Communication

We keep parents fully informed with session summaries, progress updates, and open communication throughout.

Getting Started

From Enquiry to First Lesson

Starting is straightforward. We aim to have every new student in their first session within the week.

01

Make an Enquiry

Tell us about your child — their age, subjects needed, current level, and what you'd like to achieve. Email enrol@ or use the contact form.

02

Free Trial Session

We offer a complimentary first session so your child can meet their tutor and we can carry out an informal assessment. No commitment required.

03

Your Plan Is Built

After the trial, we put together a learning plan: subjects, frequency, focus areas, and milestones. We share this with you before sessions begin.

04

Regular Sessions Start

Lessons begin on your agreed schedule. We review progress regularly and adapt the programme as your child develops.
