About Home Educators Tutoring

Founded by a qualified teacher who believes every child deserves learning that truly fits them.

Our Story

Built for Families Who Choose a Different Path

Home education is a courageous and deeply thoughtful choice. Parents who home educate do so because they want something different for their children — a learning environment shaped around the child, not the institution.

Home Educators Tutoring was founded to be a trusted partner in that journey. We provide the structured academic expertise that complements your family's approach, whether you follow a traditional curriculum, an eclectic method, or something entirely your own.

Our tutor works one-to-one with each learner, building genuine relationships and tailoring every session to how that child thinks, learns, and flourishes.

Your Tutor
👩‍🏫

Maria de los Angeles Bores-Monje

Founder & Lead Tutor

Maria is a qualified teacher with extensive experience supporting children across a wide range of ages and learning profiles. Her background spans both mainstream school settings and private tutoring, giving her a nuanced understanding of how children learn best in structured and flexible environments alike.

Having worked closely with home-educating families, Maria developed a deep appreciation for the home education model and the unique potential it offers children. She founded Home Educators Tutoring to provide families with a reliable, professional, and genuinely caring tutoring service — one built on the same values that lead families to home educate in the first place.

Maria brings warmth, patience, and rigour to every session. She believes that confidence and curiosity are the foundations of real academic progress, and she works hard to nurture both in every student she teaches.

How We Teach

Our Approach to Learning

We don't believe in teaching to the test. We believe in teaching the child. Exams matter — but so does genuine understanding, confidence, and a lifelong relationship with learning.

🎯

Start With the Learner

Every new student begins with an informal assessment so we understand where they are now, what gaps exist, and what they're capable of with the right support.

🗺️

Build a Clear Plan

We create a structured learning programme with realistic milestones, shared openly with parents so you always know what's happening and why.

🔄

Adapt Continuously

No plan survives first contact unchanged. We review and adapt regularly, responding to how your child is progressing and what's working best.
